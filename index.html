VotePledge Web App

My personal Python Flask project from March - May 2016, VotePledge allows users to pledge their vote with only an email. They are confirmed and given information about registration, as well as a reminder email closer to voting day. This mimicks real campaigns' use of post cards sent back to voters, statistically increasing voter turnout.
